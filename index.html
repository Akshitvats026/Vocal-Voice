Look for logical fallacies, unsupported claims, or contradictions in your opponent's argument. Address these systematically to weaken their position.

Personal Attacks

Avoid attacking the person making the argument. Focus on the argument itself. When opponents use ad hominem, point it out to show their weakness.

Misrepresentation

Don't misrepresent your opponent's argument to make it easier to attack. Address their actual position, not a distorted version of it.
